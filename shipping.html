Once your order ships, we will send you a tracking number via WhatsApp so you can follow your package every step of the way.

If your package doesn't arrive within 30 days, contact us and we'll either resend your order or give you a full refund.

Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping times and costs may vary by location.
