↩️ Returns & Refunds

Your satisfaction is our priority

✅ We Accept Returns If

  • Item arrived damaged or defective
  • Wrong item was sent to you
  • Item is significantly different from description
  • Package never arrived after 30 days

❌ We Cannot Accept Returns If

  • You changed your mind after ordering
  • Item doesn't fit (please check size guide before ordering)
  • Item was used or washed
  • More than 30 days have passed since delivery

💰 Refund Process

If your return is approved, here's what happens:

Step 1 — Contact us on WhatsApp with your order details and photos of the issue

Step 2 — We review your case within 24–48 hours

Step 3 — If approved, we will either:
• Resend the correct item for free, OR
• Issue a full refund to your original payment method

Step 4 — Refunds take 5–10 business days to appear

⚠️ Please do NOT return items without contacting us first. Unauthorized returns cannot be processed.

📏 Size Issues

Since our products come from international suppliers, sizes may differ slightly from standard sizing. We strongly recommend:

  • Check the size chart on each product before ordering
  • Message us on WhatsApp if you're unsure about sizing
  • When in doubt, order one size up
